Monkey D. Luffy, also known as Straw Hat Luffy, is the main protagonist of the One Piece franchise.He is now known as Joy Boy and Sun God Nika.He is the captain of the Straw Hat Pirates thatwants to find the One Piece treasure and become theKing of the Pirates. He is also the son of Monkey D. Dragon, the grandson of Monkey D. Garp and the adoptive younger brother of Portgas D. Ace and Sabo.

Zoro

Strawhat Pirates

Roronoa Zoro, also known as Pirate Hunter Zoro, is one of the main characters of the One Piece franchise. He is a swordsman and combatant of the Straw Hat Pirates and one of the twelve "Worst Generation" pirates along with his captain. He is a pirate, former bounty hunter, as well as is one of the most versatile fighters in the series and to considered one of the biggest threats by the World Government in the crew after his captain, Monkey D. Luffy, and Jinbe. After Luffy rise as one of the Four Emperors, Zoro is widely known as the "No.2 of the Emperor".

Nami

Strawhat Pirates

Nami, also known as "Cat Burglar" Nami, is one of the main characters in the One Piece franchise. She and Nico Robin are the only female members of the Straw Hat Pirates so far and is considered to be the main heroine of the series. She is the navigator of the crew. Her bounty was 16,000,000 Berries after she defeated Kalifa and declared war against the world government. Her second bounty is 66,000,000 Berries after the events in Dressrosa.

Sanji

Strawhat Pirates

Sanji, born as Vinsmoke Sanji, and also known as "Black Leg Sanji", is one of the main characters of the One Piece franchise. He is a pirate and the chef of the Straw Hat Pirates. He is the fifth member of the crew and the fourth to join. He is the third son of the Vinsmoke Family, but he later renounced his title and disowned his family twice after leaving Germa 13 years ago and after Big Mom's treachery toward the Vinsmoke Family.

Ussop

Strawhat Pirates

Usopp, known as Sogeking and "God" Usopp, is one of the main characters of the One Piece franchise. He serves as the sniper of the Straw Hat Pirates, and the former Captain of the Usopp Pirates. He is the son of Yasopp (the sniper of the Red Haired Pirates). When Usopp was first introduced, he was often (comically) labeled as the "Liar" of the Straw Hat Pirates, due to the "Uso" (うそ) in his name meaning "lie" or "falsehood".
